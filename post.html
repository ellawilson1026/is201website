Park City Ski Resort

Largest ski resort in North America

Park City Ski Resort is a renowned winter destination located in Park City, Utah. Nestled in the majestic Wasatch Mountains, it offers a world-class skiing and snowboarding experience. With a rich history dating back to the mining era, Park City Ski Resort has transformed into one of the largest and most popular ski resorts in the United States. Boasting over 7,300 acres of skiable terrain, the resort offers a diverse range of slopes catering to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced riders. Its abundant powder snow, breathtaking alpine scenery, and well-maintained trails attract winter sports enthusiasts from around the globe. Beyond skiing and snowboarding, visitors can enjoy a multitude of other activities, including snowshoeing, snowmobiling, and even a thrilling zip line adventure. Park City itself is a vibrant mountain town that offers a wide array of accommodations, restaurants, and shops, providing a charming and convenient base for visitors. Whether seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures on the slopes or a cozy mountain retreat, Park City Ski Resort is a premier destination for winter enthusiasts.
